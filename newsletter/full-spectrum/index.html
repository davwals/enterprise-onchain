Goldman Sachs files for its first Bitcoin ETF

Goldman filed with the SEC for the Goldman Sachs Bitcoin Premium Income ETF, a covered-call strategy on spot BTC ETFs. It marks the firm’s first direct Bitcoin product following two years of institutional distribution via third-party issuers.

HSBC launches tokenized deposit service in the US

HSBC’s Tokenized Deposit Service (TDS) went live for US corporate and institutional clients, enabling 24/7 cross-border transfers on a permissioned ledger. Initial corridors cover USD and HKD.

Hong Kong SFC enables 24/7 tokenised fund trading

The Securities and Futures Commission published a framework for round-the-clock secondary trading of tokenised funds on licensed platforms. The first eligible products are three tokenised money market funds.
