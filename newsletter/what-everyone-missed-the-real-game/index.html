OK, so what the hell is “cross-margining,” and why should you care?

Here’s the deal: right now, if you’re trading both stocks and crypto, you need separate piles of cash for each. It’s like having different gift cards for different stores.

Super inefficient.

Cross-margining = ONE pile of money that works EVERYWHERE.

Hidden Road + RLUSD is making this possible for the first time ever.

Some stats to blow your mind:

Institutional traders typically lock up 2-3x more capital than necessary because of this problem

Hidden Road clears over $3 TRILLION annually (yes, with a T)

They have 300+ institutional clients who will now be using RLUSD

Translation: Ripple just secured a built-in customer base for their stablecoin that makes USDC and USDT jealous.
